Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to develop a custom system?

Development timelines vary based on complexity. A basic system typically takes 4-8 weeks, while more complex enterprise solutions may take 3-6 months. We provide detailed timelines during our initial consultation.

Do you provide ongoing support after development?

Yes, we offer comprehensive maintenance and support packages. These include regular updates, bug fixes, security patches, and technical support to ensure your system continues to perform optimally.

Can you integrate with our existing systems?

Absolutely. We specialize in system integration and can connect your new system with existing software, databases, APIs, and third-party services to create a seamless operational environment.

What industries do you serve?

We serve multiple industries including telematics, tourism & hospitality, logistics & transportation, regulatory compliance, and general business operations. Our solutions are adaptable to any industry with specific operational needs.

How do you handle data security and privacy?

We implement industry-standard security practices including data encryption, secure authentication, regular security audits, and compliance with data protection regulations. Security is built into every layer of our systems.
